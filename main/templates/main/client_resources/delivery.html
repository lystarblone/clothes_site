Delivery Information

At Polaris Aurora, we understand the importance of receiving your stylish winter wardrobe and ski gear on time and in perfect condition. Whether you're shopping from Edinburgh, elsewhere in the UK, or anywhere in the world, we are committed to providing a seamless delivery experience tailored to your needs.

Delivery Methods

  • Standard Delivery: Free for orders over £50. For orders below £50, a flat rate of £4.99 applies. Delivery time is 3–5 business days. (Fulfilled via Royal Mail or DPD.)
  • Express Delivery: £14.99. This premium option ensures your order arrives within 1–2 business days. Ideal for last-minute purchases or urgent needs. (Fulfilled via DPD Express.)
  • International Delivery: Available for customers outside the UK. Shipping costs vary depending on the destination:
    • Europe: Starting from £20. Estimated delivery time is 5–7 business days.
    • North America: Starting from £30. Estimated delivery time is 7–10 business days.
    • Rest of the World: Starting from £45. Estimated delivery time is 10–14 business days.
    We partner with trusted carriers such as DHL International and FedEx to ensure reliable delivery of your items. Please note that customs duties and taxes may apply for international orders, and these fees are the responsibility of the customer.
All orders are carefully packed to ensure your items arrive in pristine condition. Our packaging is designed to withstand weather conditions during transit, so you can trust that your Polaris Aurora products will be as flawless as when they left our warehouse.
