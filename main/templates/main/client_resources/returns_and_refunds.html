Returns and Refunds

At Polaris Aurora, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free returns and refunds process. Below, you’ll find all the details about our return policy, including conditions, steps to follow, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Return Conditions

  • Timeframe: You can return items within 30 days of receiving your order.
  • Condition: Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original packaging with all tags attached.
  • Proof of Purchase: A receipt or order confirmation is required for all returns.
  • Exclusions: Personalized or custom-made items are non-returnable unless they are defective or incorrect.

How to Return an Item

  1. Contact Us: Reach out to our customer service team at info@polarisaurora.com or call us at +44 131 496 5555 to initiate your return.
  2. Provide Details: Include your order number, the items you wish to return, and the reason for the return.
  3. Pack Your Item: Place the item(s) in their original packaging along with the receipt or order confirmation.
  4. Ship It Back: Use the prepaid return label provided by our team (if applicable) or send the package to the address provided.
  5. Track Your Return: Once we receive your package, we will process your return within 3–5 business days.

Refund Process

  • Refund Method: Refunds will be issued using the original payment method. For example:
    • Credit/Debit Cards: The refund will appear on your statement within 5–7 business days.
    • PayPal/Digital Wallets: The refund will be credited to your account instantly.
    • Cryptocurrency Payments: Refunds will be issued in the same cryptocurrency used for the purchase.
  • Processing Time: Please allow up to 5 business days for the refund to reflect in your account.
  • Shipping Costs: Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to an error on our part (e.g., wrong item shipped).

Exchanges

If you’d like to exchange an item for a different size or color, follow these steps:

  1. Return the original item using the process described above.
  2. Place a new order for the desired item on our website.
  3. Contact our support team if you need assistance with the exchange process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I return an item after 30 days?

A: Unfortunately, we cannot accept returns after 30 days from the date of delivery. However, if there is a defect or issue with the product, please contact us immediately.

Q: What if the item I received is damaged or incorrect?

A: We apologize for the inconvenience! Please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving your order, and we will arrange a replacement or refund at no additional cost.

Q: Do I have to pay for return shipping?

A: Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to an error on our part (e.g., wrong item shipped or damaged goods).

Q: How long does it take to process a refund?

A: Once we receive your returned item, it typically takes 3–5 business days to process the refund. Depending on your bank, it may take an additional 5–7 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

Q: Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

A: Yes! While we don’t offer direct exchanges, you can return the item and place a new order for the desired product. Our team is happy to assist you with this process.
